Provincial Third Prize
Mathematical modeling in the three eastern provinces

"Northeast Mathematical Modeling League" is a science and technology event for college students and postgraduates jointly sponsored by universities in Heilongjiang, Jilin and Liaoning provinces. The purpose of this tournament is to further popularize mathematical modeling education, train students' ability to grasp new knowledge, obtain information quickly and apply their knowledge to solve practical problems, stimulate students' enthusiasm for independent learning, cultivate teamwork spirit, and more importantly, train students' logical thinking ability and open thinking ability, so as to improve students' ability to write scientific and technical papers, computer application ability, innovation and practical ability. The comprehensive quality of students' scientific and technical paper writing ability, computer application ability, innovation and practical ability. The Northeastern Provinces Mathematical Modeling League, together with the May 1st Mathematical Modeling League, the Central China Mathematical Modeling League and the East China Mathematical Modeling League, have become the four major regional competitions in the field of mathematical modeling in China.

International Third Prize
American college mathematical modeling competition

The Mathematical Modeling Competition for Undergraduates (MCM/ICM), sponsored by the Mathematical Consortium of America and its Applications, is the only international mathematical modeling competition and the most influential mathematical modeling competition in the world. The competition covers a wide range of topics in economics, management, environment, resources, ecology, medicine, security, and many other fields. The competition requires a team of three students (undergraduates) to complete all the work from modeling, solving, validating to writing papers on the specified problems in four days, which reflects the ability of the participants to study the problems, solutions and teamwork spirit. It is the originator of all kinds of mathematical modeling competitions today.
